# Day 2 - Operators

## What I Learned
- Operators are used to perform calculations
- Arithmetic operators include +, -, *, /, //, %, **
- % gives the remainder
- // removes decimals (floor division)

## Exercises

- Built a salary calculator with tax computation
- Practiced mathematical operations like square and cube
